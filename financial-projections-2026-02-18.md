## Section 11: Validation & Sanity Checks

### Revenue Sanity Check

✅ **Month 12 T2 subscribers (289)** — requires converting only 0.4% of the 66,550-business SAM. Extremely achievable.

✅ **Month 12 T3 rate (10/month)** — requires 40 qualified conversations per month at 25% close rate. Achievable with 2 hrs/day of outreach.

✅ **Tier 2 ACV ($1,788/yr)** — benchmarks: Jobber ($1,500–2,400), Jane App (~$1,800), Weave ($2,400). In range.

✅ **Year 3 ARR ($3.25M)** — below Jobber/Jane Year 3 benchmarks ($10M+) but those had VC capital. Conservative for a bootstrapped-to-funded path.

### Risk Factors to Monitor Monthly

| Risk | Warning Signal | Response |
|------|---------------|----------|
| Churn exceeds 15% | Less than 85% of T2 customers billing in Month 2 | Improve onboarding, add check-in call |
| T3 pipeline dries up | <2 strategy sessions booked/week | Pivot outreach channel, test new script |
| CAC exceeds $300 | Ad spend >$1,500 for <5 new customers | Shift to content/organic, cold email |
| Burn exceeds $5K/mo | Cash balance below $50K with no revenue | Pause ads, double down on T3 (higher margin) |

### Benchmark Comparisons

| Company | Stage | ARR at Year 1 | Team | Similarity |
|---------|-------|--------------|------|-----------|
| **Jobber** | Bootstrapped Y1 | ~$200K | 3 | Home services SaaS |
| **Jane App** | Bootstrapped Y1 | ~$150K | 2 | Wellness vertical SaaS |
| **AAA (base)** | Pre-seed funded | **$380K** | 1 | AI + SMB automation |

AAA projects higher Y1 revenue than Jobber/Jane because of the high-ticket Tier 3 revenue stream — a unique hybrid model those companies didn't have.
