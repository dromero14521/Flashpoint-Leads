# Customer Acquisition Playbook

This guide outlines strategies for finding, attracting, and converting customers for the AAA Platform.

---

## Target Customer Profile

### Ideal Customer Avatar (ICA)

**Primary**: Small business owners and solopreneurs who:

- Generate $50K-$500K annual revenue
- Spend 10+ hours/week on manual operations
- Use 3+ SaaS tools that don't talk to each other
- Have tried automation but failed or gave up
- Value time over money (willing to pay for solutions)

**Secondary**: Digital agencies and consultants who:

- Serve multiple clients with similar needs
- Want to productize their services
- Need to scale without adding headcount

### Industries with Highest LTV

Focus on these high-value niches:

| Industry | Pain Points | Ticket Size |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| **E-commerce** | Order fulfillment, inventory, customer service | $2,500-5,000 |
| **Real Estate** | Lead follow-up, transaction coordination | $2,500-5,000 |
| **Coaching/Consulting** | Client onboarding, scheduling, delivery | $2,500-5,000 |
| **SaaS/Tech** | Customer success, churn prevention | $5,000-10,000 |
| **Agencies** | Project management, client reporting | $2,500-7,500 |

### Red Flags (Avoid These Customers)

- Revenue under $30K/year (can't afford solutions)
- "I just need a simple template"
- Unwilling to articulate their pain
- Looking for free solutions only
- No existing tech stack (nothing to integrate)

## Acquisition Channels

### 1. Content Marketing (Long-term Authority)

**The "Cost of Pain" Framework**

Create content that quantifies the cost of NOT automating:

#### Blog Post Ideas

- "The Hidden $24,000/Year Tax You're Paying on Manual Data Entry"
- "Why Your Spreadsheet is Killing Your Business (And What to Do Instead)"
- "I Automated My Client Onboarding: Here's What Happened"
- "The 5 Workflows Every [Industry] Owner Should Automate First"

#### Content Calendar Template

| Week | Topic | Format | Distribution |
|------|-------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | Pain point article | Blog | LinkedIn, Twitter |
| 2 | Case study | Blog + Video | YouTube, LinkedIn |
| 3 | Tutorial | Video | YouTube, TikTok |
| 4 | Behind-the-scenes | Thread/Carousel | Twitter, Instagram |

#### SEO Keywords to Target

- "business automation consultant"
- "[industry] automation"
- "how to automate [specific task]"
- "zapier alternative for [industry]"
- "n8n workflows for [industry]"

### 2. Social Media (Daily Visibility)

**LinkedIn Strategy (Highest ROI for B2B)**

1. **Profile optimization**:
   - Headline: "I help [niche] owners automate [pain] | Saved clients 10+ hrs/week"
   - Banner: Clear value proposition with CTA
   - Featured: Lead magnet or case study

2. **Content rhythm**:
   - Monday: Industry insight/trend
   - Wednesday: Before/after automation story
   - Friday: Personal journey/lesson learned

3. **Engagement strategy**:
   - Comment on 10 posts from target customers daily
   - DM new connections with value (not pitch)
   - Share wins from Tier 3 implementations

**Twitter/X Strategy**

- Build in public: Share development progress
- Thread format: "I just automated [X] for a client. Here's the full breakdown:"
- Engage in #nocode and #automation communities

### 3. YouTube (Demonstration Authority)

**Video Content Framework**

1. **Problem videos** (top of funnel)
   - "5 Signs Your Business Needs Automation"
   - "The REAL Cost of Manual Processes"

2. **Solution videos** (middle of funnel)
   - "How I Built a $5K Automation for [Client Type]"
   - "Complete Workflow Walkthrough: [Specific Automation]"

3. **Product videos** (bottom of funnel)
   - AAA Platform demo
   - Customer testimonial/case study
   - "How to Get Started with AAA"

### 4. Partnerships (Accelerated Growth)

**Referral Partner Types**

| Partner Type | What They Get | What You Get |
|--------------|---------------|---------------|
| VAs/OBMs | Tool to serve clients better | Warm introductions |
| Business coaches | Solution for client ops problems | Revenue share (20-30%) |
| Accountants/Bookkeepers | Automation for their clients | Qualified referrals |
| SaaS vendors | Integration partner | Access to user base |

**Partnership Outreach Template**

```text
Subject: Partnership opportunity - [Their Company] x AAA

Hi [Name],

I noticed you work with [target audience] on [their service]. 

I've been helping similar businesses automate their [specific pain point] 
and thought there might be an opportunity to collaborate.

The typical result: [specific outcome, e.g., "10+ hours saved per week"]

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to explore if there's a fit?

[Your name]
```

### 5. Cold Outreach (Direct Acquisition)

**Identifying Prospects**

Use these signals to find automation-ready businesses:

1. **Job postings**: Companies hiring for administrative roles
2. **Tech stack signals**: Using Zapier, but posting about problems
3. **Growth signals**: Recently funded, expanding team
4. **Pain signals**: Complaining about operations on social media

**Cold Email Framework**

```text
Subject: Quick question about [their company]

Hi [Name],

I noticed [specific observation about their business].

I work with [similar companies] to automate [specific workflow], 
typically saving 10-15 hours per week.

For example, I recently helped [similar company] automate their 
[specific process], which eliminated [specific pain].

Would it make sense to chat for 15 minutes this week?

[Your name]

P.S. Even if it's not a fit, I'm happy to share the framework we used—
it might give you some ideas.
```

**Follow-up Sequence**

- Day 0: Initial email
- Day 3: Reply to own email with additional value
- Day 7: New angle/different pain point
- Day 14: Breakup email ("Is now not the right time?")

## Lead Magnets

### High-Converting Lead Magnets

1. **Automation Audit Template**
   - Self-assessment checklist
   - Identifies top automation opportunities
   - Captures pain points for follow-up

2. **ROI Calculator**
   - Input hours spent on manual tasks
   - Output: Annual cost of those hours
   - Capture email for full report

3. **Free Blueprint (Limited)**
   - One free blueprint generation
   - Requires account creation
   - Upsell to paid tiers

4. **Industry-Specific Guides**
   - "The E-commerce Automation Stack"
   - "The Consultant's Automation Playbook"
   - PDF download with email capture

### Landing Page Formula

```text
[Headline: Specific transformation]
[Subhead: How + timeframe]
[Social proof: Logos or testimonials]
[Bullet points: 3-5 benefits]
[CTA: Clear next step]
[Objection handling: FAQ or guarantee]
```
