Guidelines
Usage Rules
Keep the mark consistent across every touchpoint.

Do

  • Use the gold gradient version on dark backgrounds as the primary treatment
  • Maintain clear space equal to the F crossbar height around the mark
  • Use the Badge version for app icons, favicons, and social avatars
  • Use flat black or white versions for single-color applications
  • Scale proportionally — never stretch or compress

Don't

  • Place the gold version on light/white backgrounds (low contrast)
  • Add drop shadows, outer glows, or bevel effects
  • Rotate, skew, or apply perspective transforms
  • Change the gold palette tones to other hues
  • Use below 16px — switch to the Badge version for tiny sizes
  • Place on busy photography without a dark overlay

File Formats

  • SVG — Primary format for web and digital (scalable)
  • PNG @2x — For contexts where SVG isn't supported
  • Favicon — Use Badge concept at 32x32 and 16x16
  • Print — Export SVG at 300dpi minimum, CMYK if offset printing
