The Architect plan is billed monthly at $99/month. Your subscription begins the day you sign up and renews automatically each month on the same date. You can cancel at any time from your account dashboard.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) as well as ACH bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store card details on our servers.
