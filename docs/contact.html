Answers to the most frequently asked questions.

You can cancel your Architect subscription at any time from your account settings dashboard. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

Email support@flashpointleads.com with "Refund Request" as the subject, your account email, and the transaction date. Please review our Refund Policy to understand eligibility before submitting. We process all eligible refunds within 5 business days.

Fill out the form above and select "Sales — Apex Package" as your subject, or email us directly. We'll schedule a free 30-minute discovery call to understand your business, discuss the scope of implementation, and confirm the Apex package is the right fit before you commit to anything.
