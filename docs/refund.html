Refund & Cancellation Policy

Flashpoint Leads LLC  |  Last updated: March 22, 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy describes your rights and options regarding cancellations, refunds, and billing disputes for all Flashpoint Leads products and services. This policy is incorporated into and governed by our Terms of Service.

Summary (for quick reference): Monthly plans — cancel anytime, no mid-period refunds. Annual plans — pro-rated refund within 30 days. Apex one-time package — full refund within 48 hours if work hasn't started; no refunds once implementation begins.

1. Monthly Subscription Plans (Architect)

1.1 Cancellation

You may cancel your monthly Architect subscription at any time through your account settings dashboard or by contacting us at support@flashpointleads.com.

  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
  • You retain full access to all paid features until the billing period expires.
  • No further charges will be made after the cancellation date.
  • Your account will automatically revert to the Explorer (free) plan upon expiry.

1.2 Refunds

Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable for the current billing period. We do not issue partial refunds for unused days within a billing cycle.

Exceptions may be made at our sole discretion in cases of documented service unavailability exceeding 24 consecutive hours or billing errors caused by our systems. To report a billing error, contact us within 14 days of the charge.

1.3 Billing Errors

If you believe you have been charged incorrectly, please contact us at support@flashpointleads.com within 30 days of the charge with your account email, the transaction date, and a description of the error. We will investigate and, if a billing error is confirmed, issue a full refund of the erroneous charge.

2. Annual Subscription Plans

2.1 Cancellation

Annual subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. Cancellation prevents automatic renewal at the end of the annual term. You retain access to the Service for the remainder of the paid annual period.

2.2 Refunds

If you cancel an annual subscription and request a refund:

  • Within 30 days of the annual renewal date: You are eligible for a pro-rated refund for the unused portion of your subscription (calculated by dividing the annual fee by 12 and multiplying by the number of complete unused months remaining).
  • After 30 days of the annual renewal date: No refund is available. You may cancel to prevent future renewal charges.

Pro-rated refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5–10 business days via Stripe.

3. Apex One-Time Implementation Package ($2,500)

3.1 Cancellation Before Work Begins

You are eligible for a full refund of the $2,500 Apex package fee if:

  • Your refund request is submitted within 48 hours of the purchase timestamp, AND
  • Implementation work has not yet commenced.

"Implementation work" is defined as any activity by our team on your project, including the initial strategy session, discovery call, system access, or any deliverable preparation.

3.2 After Work Has Begun

No refunds are available once implementation work has begun, regardless of the time elapsed since purchase. This policy reflects the significant time, expertise, and resources invested from project inception.

3.3 What Constitutes "Work Beginning"

Work is considered to have begun upon the earliest of:

  • The completion of your onboarding questionnaire
  • Your first strategy or discovery session with our team
  • Any access granted to your systems, CRM, or data
  • Any written or verbal communication from our team indicating project initiation

3.4 Scope Disputes

If you believe deliverables were not completed as described in your project agreement, please contact us within 30 days of the agreed completion date. We will work in good faith to resolve disputes and, where appropriate, provide additional services to fulfill the agreed scope.

4. Free Trial

The Architect plan 14-day free trial does not require a credit card and incurs no charges. No refund requests are applicable to the free trial period as no payment is collected.

If you provide payment details to upgrade during or after a trial and are charged in error (e.g., an unexpected automatic charge), please contact us within 7 days and we will resolve the issue promptly.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please submit your request via one of the following methods:

  • Email: support@flashpointleads.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
  • Contact Form: flashpointleads.com/contact.html

Your refund request should include:

  • Your account email address
  • The date of the charge and the amount
  • The reason for your refund request
  • Any relevant Stripe transaction ID (found in your emailed receipt)

We will acknowledge your request within 1 business day and provide a resolution within 5 business days. Approved refunds are processed through Stripe and typically appear on your original payment method within 5–10 business days depending on your card issuer.

6. Stripe Payment Disputes & Chargebacks

We strongly encourage you to contact us directly at support@flashpointleads.com before initiating a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or card issuer. Most billing issues can be resolved quickly and amicably through direct communication.

6.1 Chargeback Process

If a chargeback is filed against a legitimate charge:

  • Your account will be suspended immediately pending investigation.
  • We will submit evidence of service delivery to Stripe and your bank in accordance with card network chargeback rules.
  • If the chargeback is ruled in favor of Flashpoint Leads LLC, your account suspension may become permanent.

6.2 Disputed Charges

Stripe handles all payment disputes in accordance with their Terms of Service. By using our Service, you agree to Stripe's dispute resolution process. We cooperate fully with Stripe's dispute and fraud prevention processes.

6.3 Unauthorized Transactions

If you believe an unauthorized transaction was made using your payment information, contact your card issuer immediately and then notify us at support@flashpointleads.com. We take fraud seriously and will assist with any investigation.

7. Service Credits

In cases where a full refund is not applicable but we acknowledge a failure in service delivery, we may offer service credits at our discretion. Service credits are applied to your account balance and deducted from future billing cycles. Credits are non-transferable and have no cash value.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund & Cancellation Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification at least 14 days before taking effect. Your continued use of the Service after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For refund requests, billing questions, or disputes, reach us at:

  • Business Name: Flashpoint Leads LLC
  • Email: support@flashpointleads.com
  • Response Time: Within 1 business day for refund inquiries
  • Contact Form: flashpointleads.com/contact.html
